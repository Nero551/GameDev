# 🧙 Class System

## Fighter

- +100% melee damage

- +50% attack speed

- +60% movement speed

- +60% stamina

- +30% HP

- +30% jump height

- +50% dash distance

- - Limited to augmented magic

- Can use combat styles
---

## Swordsman

- +100% sword damage
- +50% movement speed
- +30% stamina
- +50% dash distance
- +20% jump height
- Crit system:
  - Base: 5%
  - Max: 25%
  - Crit damage: 2x
- Limited to augmented magic
- Specialized in swords

---

## Witch

- +100% magic damage
- +50% mana capacity
- +50% mana regen
- -50% HP
- -30% stamina
- +50% potion effectiveness
- Full access to magic
- Uses staffs
