---
# 🧠 Core Concept

Humans possess a special organ (likely the heart) that converts glucose into **Mana**, similar to how ATP works biologically.

- **Mana = ATP**

- Used to cast spells and abilities

- If the organ fails → the character dies
---

# ⚔️ Core Gameplay Loop

1. Enter a world (universe)
2. Fight enemies
3. Collect materials
4. Unlock abilities (books / quests)
5. Craft gear & potions
6. Defeat bosses
7. Progress to next world

---

# 🔋 Mana System

- Mana is biologically generated
- Used for:
  - Spells
  - Abilities

### Potential Extensions

- Mana regeneration tied to stamina/health
- Mana overload system:
  - Overuse → fatigue, debuffs, or damage

---

# ⚔️ Combat System

## Core Mechanics

- Melee + Magic hybrid combat
- Dodge system (timing-based)

## Stamina System

- 4 stamina bars (base)
- Each dodge consumes 1 bar
- Proper timing required to evade attacks

---

# 🧩 Ability System

- Max equipped abilities: **8**
- Additional total slots: up to **12**

## Main Ability Mechanic

- One ability is designated as **Main**
- Buffs all abilities in the same category

## Categories (Examples)

- Fire
- Earth
- Combat
- Support

---

# 📖 Spell System

## Unlocking

- Learn spells by:
  - Reading books
  - Deciphering texts
- Starts a **Spell Unlock Quest**

## Structure

- Each element has **5 tiers**
- Each tier contains multiple spells

### Example:

- Tier 1 Fire
- Tier 2 Fire
- Tier 3 Fire
- Tier 4 Fire
- Tier 5 Fire

## Progression

- Element mastery system

---

# 🧙 Class System

## Fighter

- +100% melee damage
- +50% attack speed
- +60% movement speed
- +60% stamina
- +30% HP
- +30% jump height
- +50% dash distance
- - Limited to augmented magic
- Can use combat styles

---

## Swordsman

- +100% sword damage
- +50% movement speed
- +30% stamina
- +50% dash distance
- +20% jump height
- Crit system:
  - Base: 5%
  - Max: 25%
  - Crit damage: 2x
- Limited to augmented magic
- Specialized in swords

---

## Witch

- +100% magic damage
- +50% mana capacity
- +50% mana regen
- -50% HP
- -30% stamina
- +50% potion effectiveness
- Full access to magic
- Uses staffs

---

# 🛡️ Equipment System

## Weapons

- **Swords**
  - Increase sword damage
  - Increase crit chance
- **Staffs**
  - Boost magic damage
  - Increase mana regen & capacity
  - May include exclusive abilities
- **Combat Styles**
  - Unarmed combat variations

## Armor

- Buffs specific ability categories

## Tradeoff System

- Equipping items reduces available ability slots
- Encourages build specialization

---

# 🧪 Crafting & Alchemy

## Crafting

- Materials from:
  - Enemies
  - Environment
- Used for:
  - Weapons
  - Armor

## Potions

- Created via **alchemy system**
- Mix extracted chemicals from natural materials

---

# 🌍 World System

- Multiple universes (worlds)
- Each world has:
  - Unique environment
  - Unique enemies
  - Story quests

## Progression

- Story quests provide enough XP for next world

---

# 📈 Difficulty System

## World Level

- Increase difficulty manually
- Rewards:
  - Higher drop rates
  - Better loot

---

# 📦 Loot System

## Sources

- Enemies
- Bosses
- Chests (respawn over time)

## Boss Drops

- Used for crafting powerful gear

---

# ⚡ Status Resistance System

- Gain resistance by repeated exposure

### Example:

- Poison Resistance:
  - Low: 10 hits
  - Moderate: 100 hits

---

# 🎮 Additional Systems

## Passive Skills

- Crafting
- Forging
