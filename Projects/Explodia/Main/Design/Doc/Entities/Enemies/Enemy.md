An Enemy Has 3 important things to focus on when fighting,
- Level
- Health
- Arts
- Drops

An enemy's level is determined by the [[World Level]] and location of the enemy.
if the enemy is in a high level area not meant for newbies , he will be significantly stronger.

Health is chosen randomly from a defined number range  (e.x 100-150). That number is then applied to the level using this formula: health * (level  / 3.5).

Arts are predefined chosen by me.

Drops are also predefined by me, of course you are not guaranteed to get all the drops.
