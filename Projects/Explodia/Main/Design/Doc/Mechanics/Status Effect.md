
## Effects can be:
  - Instant
  - Duration-based
  - Stackable or non-stackable

## Categories:
  - Damage over time(DOT)
  - Crowd control
  - Buffs
  - Debuffs
  - Modifiers (Pierce, Crit Boost, etc.)

## Priority Rules:
  - Stronger effect overrides weaker
  - Some effects cannot stack

## Cleanup:
  - Effects must define removal conditions
