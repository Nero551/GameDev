## Table of Contents

### Arts

- [Art](Arts/Art.md) — A unique attack a person can cast; players can have 10 arts (9 normal, 1 Ultimate). Uses Mana or Stamina; damage scales with main stats.
- [Ultimate Art](Arts/Ultimate Art.md) — An individual's ULTIMATE attack. Very few can achieve this level of might.

### Damage Types

- [Physical](Damage Types/Physical.md) — Uses Stamina.
- [Magic](Damage Types/Magic.md) — Uses Mana.

### Mechanics

- [Critical](Mechanics/Critical.md) — A chance to multiply a player's damage on hit; affected by Critical Rate and Critical Damage.
- [Black Fläsh](Mechanics/Black Fläsh.md) — If Critical Rate > 150% there's a small chance to trigger a powerful double-damage effect.
- [Dodge](Mechanics/Dodge.md) — (no content)

### Resources

- [Health](Resources/Health.md) — An individual's lifeforce.
- [Stamina](Resources/Stamina.md) — Primary resource for Physical attacks.
- [Mana](Resources/Mana.md) — Primary resource for Magic attacks.

### Stats

- **Main stats**
  - [Strength](Stats/Main stats/Strength.md) — Increases Stamina capacity and regeneration.
  - [Vitality](Stats/Main stats/Vitality.md) — Increases Health capacity and regeneration.
  - [Dexterity](Stats/Main stats/Dexterity.md) — Increases Critical Rate & Critical Damage.
  - [Agility](Stats/Main stats/Agility.md) — Increases attack & movement speed; increases Stamina capacity and regeneration.
  - [Intelligence](Stats/Main stats/Intelligence.md) — Increases Mana capacity and regeneration.
- **Sub stats**
  - [Critical Damage](Stats/Sub Stats/Critical Damage.md) — Mainly gained by increasing Dexterity; also from Armor or boosts.
  - [Critical Rate](Stats/Sub Stats/Critical Rate.md) — Mainly gained by increasing Dexterity; also from Armor or boosts.
